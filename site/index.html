The Problem

Your wellness program is missing half the picture.

Traditional workplace wellness focuses on fitness, nutrition, and mental health — but ignores one of the most impactful areas of employee wellbeing: relationship with alcohol.

Alcohol-related workplace issues cost employers billions annually in absenteeism, reduced productivity, health claims, and turnover. Yet most companies have nothing to address it — leaving employees to navigate sobriety alone.
